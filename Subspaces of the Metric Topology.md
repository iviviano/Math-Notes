```ad-thm
Let $d$ be a [[Metric]] on $X$; $A$ be a [[Set Theory/Subset|Subset]] of $X$. Then, the [[Topology]] induced by the [[Set Theory/Restriction|Restriction]] of $d$ to $A\times A$ is the same as the [[Subspace Topology]] on $A$.
```
