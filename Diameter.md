> [!def]
> If a set $A$ in a [[Metric Space]] $X$ under the [[Metric]] $d$ is [[Bounded Metric Space]] and nonempty, the *diameter* of $A$ is defined to the number
> $$\text{diam } A = \text{ sup }\{d(a_1,a_2)|a_1, a_2\in A\}$$
> where sup is the [[Supremum]].
