>[!prop]
>Let $X$ be a [[Topological Space]]. If $C$ is a [[Connected]] [[Subset]] of $X$ and
>$$C\subseteq Y\subseteq\bar{C}$$
>then $Y$ is [[Connected]].

>[!prop] Corollary
>The [[Closure]] of a connected set is [[Connected]] and each [[Connected Component]] is [[Closed Set]].
