>[!def]
Let $\theta$ be a [[Homomorphism]] of a [[Groupoid]] $G$ into a [[Groupoid]] $H$. Then $\theta$ is called an *epimorphism* if $\theta$ maps the [[Carrier]] of $G$ onto the [[Groupoid]] $H$ ($G \theta=H$, $\theta:G \rightarrow H$ is [[Surjective]]).
