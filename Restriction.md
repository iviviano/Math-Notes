```ad-def
Let $f:A\mapsto B$ be a [[Function]] and $A_0$ be a [[Set Theory/Subset|Subset]] of $A$. The *restriction* of $f$ to $A_0$ is the [[Function]] mapping $A_0$ to $B$ whose [[Rule of Assignment]] is 
$${(a, f(a)|a\in A_0)}
```
