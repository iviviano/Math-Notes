>[!prop]
>If $X$ is [[Paracompact]] and $F$ is a [[Closed Set]] [[Subset]] of $X$, then $F$ is [[Paracompact]].

>[!proof]
Let $\mathcal{U}$ be an [[Open Cover]] of $F$. Then, $\mathcal{C}=D\cup(X-F)$ is an [[Open Cover]] of $X$.
