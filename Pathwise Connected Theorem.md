>[!thm]
Let $\{X_{i}:i\in I\}$ be a [[Collection]] of [[Topological Space]]s. Then, if the [[Cartesian Product]] $X$ is given the [[Product Topology]], $X$ is [[Pathwise Connectedness]] [[iff]] $X_i$ is [[Pathwise Connectedness]] for all $i\in I$.
