```ad-def
For the [[Function]]s $f:A\mapsto B$ and $g:B\mapsto C$, the *composition*: $g$[[composition]]$f$ is the [[Function]] [[composition]]$:A\mapsto C$ with the [[Rule of Assignment]]:
$$\{(a, c) | \text{ For some } b\in B, f(a) = b \text{ and } g(b) = c\}$$
```
