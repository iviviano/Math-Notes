mathLink: a topology is the collection of unions of its basis elements
---
```ad-prop
Let $X$ be a set, [[Notation/basis|basis]] be a [[Topology/Basis|Basis]] for a [[Topology]] $T$ on $X$. Then $T$ equals the collection of all [[Set Theory/Union|Union]]s of [[Topology/Basis|Basis]] elements.
```
