	$$\mathcal{S} = \{\pi_1^{-1}(U)|U\text{ open in }X\}\cup\{\pi_2^{-1}(V)|V\text{ open in } Y\}$$

is a [[Subbasis]] for the [[Product Topoplogy]] on $X$ [[Notation/cartesian product|cartesian product]] $Y$.
