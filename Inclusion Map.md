```ad-def
Let $X$ be a [[Topology/Topological Space|Topological Space]] and $Y$ be a [[Subspace]] of $X$. The *inclusion map* $j: Y\rightarrow X$ is defined with [[Rule of Assignment]]:
$$j(y) = y$$
```

```ad-not
Inclusion maps are sometimes denoted: $j:Y\hookrightarrow X$
```
