>[!prop]
>Let $X$ be a [[Topological Space]]. Let $f:C_{b}(X)\times C_{b}(X)\rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ be defined by the [[Rule of Assignment]]:
>$$\rho(f,g)=\sup\{|f(x)-g(x)|:x\in X\}$$
>where $f,g\in$ [[Vector Space of Continuous Bounded Functions]]$(X)$.
