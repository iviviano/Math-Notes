>[!prop]
Every $p$-[[P-Form]] on an [[Open Set]] [[Subset]] $U\subseteq \mathbb{R}^{k}$ may be uniquely expressed as a sum $$\sum _{I}f_{I}dx_{I}$$over increasing index sequences $I=(i_{1}<\cdots<i_{p})$, the $f_{I}$ being [[Function]]s on $U$.
