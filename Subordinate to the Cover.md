>[!def]
>If $\mathcal{G}$ is a [[Open Cover]] of $X$, then we say that a [[Partition of Unity]] is *subordinate to the [[Cover]]* $\mathcal{G}$ if the [[Open Cover]] $\{\{x\in X:\phi_{i}(x)>0\}:i\in I\}$ is a [[Refinement]] of $\mathcal{G}$.
