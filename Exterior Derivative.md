>[!def]
>Let $\omega=\sum a_{I}dx_{I}$ be a [[Smooth Form]] $p$-[[P-Form]] on an [[Open Set]] [[Subset]] of $\mathbb{R}^{k}$ or $H^{k}$. The *exterior derivative* of $\omega$ is defined as the $(p+1)$-[[P-Form]] $$d \omega=\sum da_{I}\land dx_{I}$$
