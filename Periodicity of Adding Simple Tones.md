>[!prop] Lemma
Given $A_{1},A_{2}\ne0$ and $\nu_{1}> \nu_{2}>0$, consider $f:\mathbb{R}\rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ given by $$f(t)=A_{1}\sin(2\pi \nu_{1}t)+A_{2}\sin(2\pi \nu_{2}t)$$Then, $f$ is [[Periodic]] [[iff]] $\frac{\nu_{1}}{\nu_{2}}$ is rational.
