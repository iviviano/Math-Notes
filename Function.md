```ad-def
A *function* f is a [[Rule of Assignment]] together with a set B such that the [[Image Set]] of r is a [[Set Theory/Subset|Subset]] of B. The [[Domain]] A of r is also called the [[Domain]] of the *function* f. The [[Image Set]] of r is also called the [[Image Set]] of f, and the set B is called the [[Range]] of f.
```
