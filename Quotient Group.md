>[!def]
Let $G$ be a [[Group]] and let $H$ be a [[Subgroup]] of $G$. The [[Collection]] of all [[Left Coset]]s and [[Right Coset]]s of $H$ in $G$ forms a [[Group]]. We call this [[Group]] the *quotient group* of $G$ by $H$ and we write it as $G/H$.
