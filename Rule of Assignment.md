> [!def]
> A *rule of assignment* is a [[Subset]] $R$ of the [[Cartesian Product]] $C$ [[notation for cartesian product]] $D$ such that each element of $C$ appears as the first coordinate of at most ordered pair belonging to $R$.

>[!note]
>$R$ is a [[Relation]].
