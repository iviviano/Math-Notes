>[!def]
>Let $S$ be a [[Set]] with an [[Equivalence Relation]] $R$. Let $x\in S$. Then, the *$R$-equivalence class* of $x$ is the [[Subset]] of $S$: $$\{y\in S:(x,y)\in R\}$$

>[!not]
>The $R$-classes of an [[Equivalence Relation]] are denoted $S/R$.
