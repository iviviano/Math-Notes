>[!prop]
>1. If $\{x_{n}\}$ is an increasing [[Sequence]] in $[0, \Omega]$ and $x=\sup\{x_{n}\}$, then $x_{n}\rightarrow x$ in the [[Order Topology]].
>2. A [[Subset]] $F$ of $[0,\Omega)$ is [[Closed Set]] [[iff]] it is sequentially closed.
