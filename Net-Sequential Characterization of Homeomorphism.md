>[!prop]
Let $X$ be a [[Topological Space]], let $\{x_{i}\}$ be a [[Net]] in $X$. The a [[Function]] $f:X \mapsto Y$ is a [[Homeomorphism]] [[iff]] whenever $\{x_{i}\}$ is a [[Net]] in $X$, $$x_{n} \rightarrow  x\iff f(x_{i})\rightarrow f(x)$$
