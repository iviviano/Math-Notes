>[!def]
>Let $\phi^{-1}:V \rightarrow U$ be a [[Coordinate System]]. Then when we write the map in coordinates:
>$$\phi^{-1}=(x_{1},\ldots,x_{k})$$
>the $k$ [[Smooth]] [[Function]]s $x_{1},\ldots,x_{k}$ are called *coordinate functions*
