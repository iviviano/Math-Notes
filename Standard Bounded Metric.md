---
```ad-def
Let $X$ be a [[Metric Space]] with [[Metric]] $d$. Define $\bar{d}:X\times X\rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ be the equation
$$\bar{d}(x, y) = \text{ min }\{d(x, y), 1\}$$
Then, $\bar{d}$ is called the *standard bounded metric* corresponding to $d$.
```
